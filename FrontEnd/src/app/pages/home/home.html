Frequently Asked Questions

When does the new tax law take effect?

The Personal Income Tax Law (Royal Decree 56/2025) takes effect from January 1, 2028.

What is the tax rate in Oman?

A flat 5% tax rate applies to annual income above OMR 42,000. The first OMR 42,000 is tax-free.

What expenses can I deduct?

Education, healthcare, mortgage interest, zakat, and charitable donations (up to 5% of income) are deductible.

When do I need to file my tax return?

Tax declarations must be submitted by June 30th for the previous tax year.

Is Flossy free to use?

Yes, Flossy offers a free tier for basic tax calculations and income tracking.

Is my financial data secure?
